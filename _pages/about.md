Hello, I'm Aryeh. I'm currently a Peter Buck Postdoctoral Fellow at the Smithsonian National Museum of Natural History, where I study genomes to understand the origins of evolutionary novelty and innovation. I completed my Ph.D. in 2026 in the Division of Biology and Biomedical Sciences at Washington University in St. Louis in the lab of [Dr. Jonathan Losos](https://sites.wustl.edu/losos/). I’ve also worked in translational genome analytics, statistical genetics, and epigenomics at Merck.
